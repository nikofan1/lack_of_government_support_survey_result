Lack of Government Support for Small Businesses in Los Angeles

This website presents a clean summary of the survey findings from 9 responses. The results point to long permit timelines, low awareness of government support programs, and a strong perception that local government has not been very helpful in the business start-up process.

Key Takeaways

Microbusiness-heavy sample

Most respondents run very small businesses, with 8 out of 9 reporting between 1 and 50 employees.

Permits often take time

Only 22% received permits in 0 to 6 months, while 78% took 6 months or longer.

Support systems feel distant

Most respondents were not familiar or only slightly familiar with local support programs, and nearly all had never applied.
